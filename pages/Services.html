What We Do

Green Earth Recycling has been the metal recycler with a smile you can count on since 1986. From peddler traffic to industrial roll off service, we have the expertise and equipment to handle any size job. You'll receive the best price for metal of all grades - from cars to cables, we've got you covered. We have grown from a small building in downtown to over 20 acres located on the city limits of Hopkinsville. Our company specializes in scrap metal recycling and processing of all types of ferrous and nonferrous scrap metal. We can handle anything from a single can to an automobile to your complex industrial project. We will weigh your items with our state certified/inspected scales and turn that into cash for you. Industrial and commercial accounts are welcome. Roll-off containers are available for delivery to your site for your larger projects. We pay competitive prices on all of your scrap metal.
